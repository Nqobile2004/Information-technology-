About Future Tech Academy

Our Story

Future Tech Academy was founded in 2018 by a group of South African software engineers and educators who saw a critical skills gap in the local tech industry. Too many talented young people lacked access to practical, affordable tech training that actually led to jobs.

We started with one classroom in Soweto and 12 students learning basic web development. Today, we’ve trained over 3,500 graduates across South Africa through our online platform, with 87% finding tech employment within 6 months of completion.

Our philosophy is simple: learn by building. Every course includes real-world projects, code reviews, and career coaching. We don’t just teach theory — we prepare you for your first day on the job.
