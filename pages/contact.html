Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a laptop for the course?
Yes, all students need their own laptop. Minimum specs: 8GB RAM, i5 processor or equivalent. We offer a laptop rental scheme for R300/month if needed.
Are your courses accredited?
Future Tech Academy is MICT SETA accredited. Our AWS and CompTIA courses also prepare you for international vendor certifications.
Can I study part-time while working?
Absolutely. All courses are designed for working professionals with evening and weekend classes, plus recorded sessions.
What is the refund policy?
Full refund if you withdraw 7 days before course start. 50% refund in the first week. No refunds after week 2.
Do you offer job placement?
We offer career support, not guaranteed placement. This includes CV reviews, mock interviews, and access to our hiring partner network. 78% of 2024 graduates found tech roles within 6 months.
Is there an age limit?
No upper age limit. Minimum age is 18. We welcome career changers of all ages.
